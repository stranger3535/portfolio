About Me

My name is Abhijith. I am a passionate and self-motivated web developer who loves learning new technologies and building clean, simple and responsive websites. I enjoy taking ideas and turning them into real interactive web pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I started my web development journey with the goal of understanding how websites work from the inside. Over time, I developed skills in frontend development, UI design, and creating user-friendly web experiences. I constantly work on improving my knowledge by practicing through projects, tutorials, and online resources.

Apart from coding, I enjoy exploring new tools, solving problems, and experimenting with creative layouts. I believe that continuous learning is the key to becoming a great developer, and I work on building something new every day.
